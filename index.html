Top three activities to do in Moscow

Explore Red Square

Visit the iconic Red Square and marvel at the majestic St. Basil's Cathedral and the Kremlin. A must-see for history lovers!

Stroll through Gorky Park

Relax in the vast green spaces of Gorky Park. Perfect for picnics, cycling, or enjoying the many events hosted throughout the year.

Immerse yourself in art at the Tretyakov Gallery

Discover the rich world of Russian art at the Tretyakov Gallery, featuring works from famous artists throughout history.
